Lemon Zucchini Bread

A zucchini bread with the added zing of lemon

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups shredded zucchini
  • .75 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • .5 cup vegetable oil
  • 1.5 cups all purpose flour
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • .5 teaspoon baking soda
  • .25 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease an 8x4 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, beat together zucchini, sugar, egg and oil.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder; stir in the cinnamon and lemon zest
  4. Stir the flour mixture into the zucchini mixture just until blended
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake 45 minutes in preheated oven, until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove from heat, and cool about 10 minutes before turning out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
