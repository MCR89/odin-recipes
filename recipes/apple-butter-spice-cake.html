Apple Butter Spice Cake

A moist cake to use up your extra apple butter.

Ingredients

Topping

  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • .5 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • .5 cup chopped pecans

Cake

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • .5 cup butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • .75 cup apple butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • .5 cup whole bran cereal or wheat germ
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs, room temperature

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  3. Prepare the topping by mixing together the brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and chopped pecans.
  4. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  5. Beat butter and sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy; add eggs one at a time, thoroughly beating each egg into the butter mixture before adding the next.
  6. Add apple butter, vanilla, and wheat germ or bran cereal
  7. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with sour cream; mix well after each addition.
  8. Pour half the batter into the prepared pan; sprinkle with half of the topping. Pour remaining batter into pan and top with rest of the topping.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean, about 40 minutes.
